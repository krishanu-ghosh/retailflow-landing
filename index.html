// data security

Your data belongs
to your business.

Every transaction, every login, every settings change is logged against the user who made it. Role-based access keeps sensitive numbers away from the wrong eyes — and your data stays private, with no background telemetry or third-party data sharing.

  • Full audit trail on every sale, void, refund, and inventory adjustment
  • 26 granular permissions — control exactly who sees what
  • Each staff member has their own login — no shared passwords
  • Financial reports visible only to the Owner role by default
  • No background telemetry or usage data sent to third parties
  • CSV export at any time — your data is always readable and portable
  • Owner-only controls for user management and business configuration
